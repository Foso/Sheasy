Sheasy Privacy Policy

Sheasy is a Free and Open Source application.
One may obtain its source code here: https://github.com/Foso/Sheasy


The app only collects data that is needed for the usage of the app.
It has no ads, no tracking and no intention in profit from you in any manner.

This app creates a HTTP Server. You can download/upload files to it over a web interface.


If you are not satisfied with this Privacy policy, please delete the app immediately.
